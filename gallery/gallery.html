Here are some of the members from this site, showing us how the skills and knowledge they have gained from this site has influenced their landscaping and design choices in their gardens.

The owner of this garden is Emily Smith. She is from Troon in Scotland and has been a member since April 2012. Her garden features a variety of exotic flowers and traditional shrubbery.

The owners of this garden are Mary and Graham Tipping. They are from Devon in England and have been members since June 2014. Their garden features a large tomato greenhouse, path edging and colourful flowers.

The owner of this garden is Peter Franklin. He is from Cardiff in Wales and has been a member since December 2010. His garden features Japanese Cherry Blossom and a luscious green lawn.

The owner of this garden is Mark Kelly. He is from Glasgow in Scotland and has been a member since February 2013. His garden features topiary shrubbery and tiered flowerbeds.

The owner of this garden is Michelle George. She is from Manchester in England and has been a member since July 2014. Her garden features a mixture of pebbles, bark chips and turf rolls.

The owner of this garden is Patrick Livingstone. He is from Brighton in England and has been a member since November 2010. His garden features hanging baskets, divided lawns and concrete planters.

The owner of this garden is Lorraine McFarland. She is from Bangor in Wales and has been a member since January 2013. Her garden features tiered lawn, separate patio area and garden furniture.

The owner of this garden is Julia Ridings. She is from Dundee in Scotland and has been a member since March 2014. Her garden features a cut-off patio area that includes furniture and a water feature.
