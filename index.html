About us

Welcome to Garden Swappers UK. We offer a large variety of useful materials for all gardening enthusiasts. Whether you’re an amateur window box gardener, or a retired tree surgeon, our site has everything you need to expand your knowledge and skills.

Our main areas of activity are organising events for our community members and providing platform for swapping plants.

Events

Become a member to take part in our monthly excursions that are held all across the UK. Information events are held regularly to bring our members together, allowing them to communicate and exchange advice and materials with one another. We have links with well-established gardening experts and landscape artists who can give you a great insight on how to start your gardening project and how to build the garden of your dreams. Allotment planning and vegetable patch volunteering programmes are held once a month to bring communities together and help to transform wasteland into useful, organic environments that will attract wildlife and harvest consumable produce right at your back door. For more information on these events, visit Events section or contact our team via the ‘Contact Us’ section of the site.

Swapping

Many bids are made daily on our website for seeds and other gardening materials on offer from our members. To make a request on any of the items available, sign in or sign up to the site and browse the products available. If you see something you like, press the “request button”- the person offering the items will then get back to you. NOTE: we do not arrange postage of the items you bid on, but we can give helpful advice on reliable couriers and postal services. To view the items on offer, please view the ‘What’s on Offer’ section of the website.

All users are encouraged to make offers themselves. For more information visit ‘Make a bid’ section.
