Frequently asked question

  1. What happens after I request a plant?

    The person who is offering the plant will be notified, and he will get in touch with you. We do not arrange delivery of seeds.

  2. Who pays for the delivery?

    You arrange it with your swap partner.

  3. What if person promising to send seeds doesn't do so?

    We believe in honesty of our members. In future users will be able to increase or decrease rating of a person they were swapping with.

  4. What is Garden Swappers Rating? How do I increase my rating?

    Rating is anticipated feature of our website. The only way to increase rating is by swapping plants. Persons obtaining higher rating will be considered "top members", so will get free access to workshops, prizes and, of course, respect from other members. Moreover, they will be able to contact persons who swapped certain plants in the past, thus chance to get a plant even if it is not currently offered. Having high rating indicates that you have rich garden, so, gardening is not a "1 day interest" for you. This will attract other experienced gardeners, and you may create great relationship.

  5. Are you selling plants?

    No, we are not a shop, and items will never be sold. Bidders may ask you to pay for delivery, which is reasonable, but that depends on your arrangements

  6. What are the benefits of swapping plants and not buying them in a nearby shop?

    • You save. Amount saved depends on a plant you are interested in, but on average it is going to be about 5£ per swap.
    • You don't have to buy standard packs of seeds, so you can replace quantity with diversity.
    • There is incredible diversity in gardens of our members. Even biggest plant shops are unable to compete with it.
    • Swapping is also about communication, meeting new people having same passion, learning from their experience, overall, making life-long friends.
