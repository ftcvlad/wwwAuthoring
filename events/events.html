Forthcoming events(Use Space to open an event with keyboard)

This event is held in Caird Hall, Dundee to showcase some of the most beautiful winter garden materials ranging from weather-proof shrubs and ice-resistant flowers for your flowerbeds. Elaine will deliver a talk on how to care for plants that are not up to surviving the winter and how to re-pot, transfer and maintain them in your home. Many useful brochures, samples of seeds and freebies will be available. Don’t worry if you can't make it, the event is recorded and will be available on our youtube channel.

Event date: 20/12/14

This event is held in Bramley Hall, London to give our members a chance to try out a wide variety of garden power tools and is an excellent way of getting to grips with the vast selection of machinery available. Robert is going to exhibit some handy tools that can be purchased from a number of local DIY stores and Garden Centres all around the UK. He will give first hand advice on how to select, use and maintain the garden power that is right for you.

Event date: 11/12/14

These meetings take place once a month in a number of locations within the UK. They give members a chance to meet up, rather than only communicating over the internet and allows them to organise events to fundraise for community gardens, memorials etc. Many people enjoy these meetings as they do not happen every week, and so the attendance is always good and the presentations are interesting. Next meeting is scheduled in the end of November.

To find out where your local meetings are held, contact us via the ‘Contact Us’ page.

Event date: 30/11/14
