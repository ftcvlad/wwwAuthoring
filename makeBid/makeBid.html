Why should you make an offer?

Sharing your garden is a fun activity in itself -- it is an opportunity to get acquainted with new people having same interest, so to share your experience and learn something new. Sending a couple of spare seeds doesn't cost you anything, but it greatly increases diversity of our gardens. With each successful bid you get points, which increase your rating, thus, trustworthiness. So, people are more likely to share something with you. Moreover, upon obtaining a certain amount of points, you can win prizes such as free trips, free master classes from best garden designers and other.

How to make an offer?

First, you need to sign in. Next, fill in the form below specifying required information. If the plant is already on our website, just input plant's ID. If you entered correct information, the offer will appear within 2 days.
